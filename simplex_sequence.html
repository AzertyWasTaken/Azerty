Simplex sequence

Home Summary

Definition

Simplex sequences are sequences that appear in the diagonals of Pascal triangle.

Triangular sequence

The triangular sequence is a sequence where each term after the first is found by adding to the previous number the number of the term.

First terms

$1, 3, 6, 10, 15, 21, 28, 36, 45, 55, ...$

Note

The sum of two consecutive terms are always square numbers.

Formula

$n\times (n+1)\div 2$

Tetrahedral sequence

The triangular sequence is a sequence where each term $n$ after the first is found by adding to the previous number the $n$th term of the triangular sequence.

First terms

$1, 4, 10, 20, 35, 56, 84, 120, 165, 220, ...$
