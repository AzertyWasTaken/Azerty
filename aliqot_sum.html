Aliquot Sum

Home Summary

Definition

The aliqot sum of an integer is the sum of its divisors.

First terms

$0, 1, 1, 3, 1, 6, 1, 7, 4, 8, 1, 16,...$

Note

1 is the only number that have exactly 1 divisor.

Square numbers divisors count is odd.

Perfect numbers

Perfect numbers are integers equal to the sum of their proper divisors.

First terms

$6, 28, 492, 8128...$
