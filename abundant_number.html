Abundant Number

Home Summary

Definition

Abundant numbers are integers strictly smaller than the sum of their proper divisors.

First terms

$12, 18, 20, 24, 30, 36, 40, 42, 48, 54,...$

Note

Most abundant numbers are even. The first abundant odd number is 945.

Deficient numbers

Deficient numbers are integers strictly larger than the sum of their proper divisors.

First terms

$1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 13,...$
