Definition

Mathematics are the abstract science of numbers, quantity, and space.

They are broken down into several areas.

Number theory

Number theory is the study of integers and arithmetic functions.

Arithmetic

Arithmetic is the study of numerical operations like addition or division.

Geometry

Geometry is the study of space and shapes.

Point

A point is a fundamental entity which represent a position in space, without size, or rotation.

Points do not have dimension. They are purely abstract objects.

Line

A line is a fundamental figure which represent a straight line infinite in both directions, without thickness.

Triangle

A triangle is a plane figure formed by 3 segments which connect 3 non aligned points called summits.

It's the three-sided polygon, and the simplest polygonal figure.
