Power sequence

Home Summary

Definition

A power sequence is a sequence where each term equal the number of the term exponentiated by a fixed number called the power.

Square sequence

The square sequence is a sequence where each term equal the number of the term exponentiated by 2.

First terms

The first square numbers are:

$1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36, 49, 64, 81, 100, ...$

Cubic sequence

The cubic sequence is a sequence where each term equal the number of the term exponentiated by 3.

First terms

The first cubic numbers are:

$1, 8, 27, 64, 125, 256, 343, 512, 729, 1024, ...$
