Evil Number

Home Summary

Definition

Evil numbers are numbers that have an even number of 1s in their binary expansion.

First terms

$0, 3, 5, 6, 9, 10, 12, 15, 17, 18, ...$

Odious numbers

Odious numbers are numbers that have an odd number of 1s in their binary expansion.

First terms

$1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 11, 13, 14, 16, 19, ...$

Note

Evil and odious sequences do not contain 3 consecutive numbers.
