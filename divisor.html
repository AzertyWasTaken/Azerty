Divisor

Home Summary

Definition

A divisor of an integer $n$ is an integer that can be divided by $n$.

First terms

$1, 2, 2, 3, 2, 4, 2, 4, 3, 4, 2, 6,...$

Note

1 is the only number that have exactly 1 divisor.

Square numbers divisors count is odd.

Multiple

A multiple of an integer $n$ is an integer that can be obtained by multiplying $n$ by any integer.
