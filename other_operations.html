Other operations

Home Summary

Modulus

Modulus return the remainder of a number divided by another number.

Modulus are written in the form $n\%k$.

Exponentiation

Exponentiation is the repeatition of multiplications. The result is called the power. This operation is not commutative.

Exponentiations are written in the form $n^k$.

Summation

Summation is the addition of a sequence of numbers defined with a function. The result is their total.

Summations are written in the form $\sum_{k=n}^{m}f(k)$.

Binomial coefficient

Binomial coefficient are written in the form $\binom{n}{k}$.

$\binom{n}{0}=1, \binom{n}{n}=1$

$\binom{n}{k}=\binom{n-1}{k}+\binom{n-1}{k-1}$
