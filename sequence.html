Sequence

Home Summary

Definition

A sequence is an ordered list of numbers, called terms.

The number of elements is called the length of the sequence.

The length of a sequence can be infinite.

Types of sequences

Arithmetic

An arithmetic sequence is a sequence where each term after the first is found by adding to the previous one a fixed number called the common difference.

They can be defined with a formula on the form $nm+k$.

Geometric

A geometric sequence is a sequence where each term after the first is found by multiplying the previous one by a fixed number called the common ratio.

They can be defined with a formula on the form $m^{n}k$.

Reccurent

A reccurent sequence is a sequence defined by a relation between several terms.

Arithmetic and geometric sequences are reccurent sequences.
